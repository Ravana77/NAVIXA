COMPANY INFORMATION

OUR STORY

Navixa Solutions Sdn Bhd is a Malaysia-based technology and management solutions company built to solve real operational challenges across multiple industries. We started with a clear understanding of the pain points faced by businesses and service providers — from high commission food delivery platforms that impact restaurants, riders, and customers, to inefficient building management processes and fragmented security systems. Through products like JomHantar, our building management systems, and integrated security solutions, we design practical, scalable platforms that reduce costs, improve efficiency, and create balanced value for all stakeholders. As a homegrown company, Navixa Solutions is committed to building technology that supports sustainable growth, empowers local businesses, and delivers smarter, fairer solutions for the Malaysian market

OUR VALUES

We believe technology should simplify operations, create fairness, and enable growth, not add complexity or unnecessary cost. At Navixa Solutions, our values guide how we build every product and partnership. We focus on transparency in pricing, efficiency in execution, and long-term value creation. Whether supporting restaurants and riders through JomHantar, streamlining operations with building management systems, or strengthening protection through security solutions, we are driven by the same principles: fairness for stakeholders, reliability in service, and innovation with purpose. We build solutions that work together helping businesses operate smarter, communities grow stronger, and technology serve people better.
